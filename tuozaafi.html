Tuo Zaafi and Ayoyo Stew

Tuo zaafi is a local dish for muslims in ghana

Ingredients

  • Ayoyo Leaves
  • Salt peter (potassium nitrate)
  • Salt
  • Okro (poured)
  • Dawada
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Seasoning
  • Fish seasoning
  • Spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato (puree)
  • Fish(salmon, herrings)
  • Cassava Flour
  • Corn Flour

Instructions

Steps to Preparing Ayoyo Stew

  1. Chop the ayoyo leaves into smaller pieces
  2. Chop onions into smaller sizes
  3. Cut meat into preferable sizes, wash and put into a cooking pot
  4. Boil water under high heat
  5. Add chopped onion, powdered fish, powdered okro and dawadawa
  6. After 5 to 7 minutes, add ayoyo leaves and salt peter (kawu)
  7. Stir continuously until it ingredients becomes very soft (do not cover)
  8. Blend onions, ginger, garlic and add to meat
  9. Simmer the chopped meat over medium heat
  10. Add salt and seasoning and cover. Steam about 10 minutes
  11. Heat saucepan over medium heat until hot and add vegetabel oil
  12. Add the chicken if any
  13. Fry until all sides are equally brown
  14. Transfer it into a boil and set aside
  15. Add chopped onion and garlic to the oil and stir until tender and starting to brown
  16. Add grinded pepper, tomato puree and stir intermittently
  17. Wash salmon and herrings and add to stew
  18. Add all spices after 10 minutes and allow to cook
  19. Add salt to taste
  20. Add chopped onions
  21. Add fried meat(chicken) and allow to simmer for 5 minutes

Steps to prepare Tuo Zaafi

  1. Pour some corn flour into cooking pot and add water
  2. Mix thoroughly to form a uniform solution
  3. Boil enough water and add corn flour to cook for 5 to 10 minutes
  4. Fetch some of the solution made earlier into a separate brown
  5. Mix dry corn dough cassava dough and add mixture to the boiling corn bit by bit
  6. Stir continuously and thoroughly to avoid lumps
  7. Add the corn dough solution you fetched (step 4) to the Tuo zaafi to make it soft and stir
  8. Turn off heat after 15 minutes of stirring
  9. Serve zaafi with stew and soup
