Groundnut Soup

Ingredients

  • Cup groundnut paste
  • 4 pieces medium-sized tomatoes
  • 4 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 pound meat(chicken, beef, fish)
  • 2 litres water
  • 3 tbsp salt
  • 5 pieces red pepper
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Wash meat thoroughly, chop and place into a clean cooking pot
  2. Add seasonings and mixture with your hands thoroughly
  3. Place over low heat and allow to steam for about 10 minutes
  4. Turn the steaming mixture from time to time
  5. Pour the groundnut paste into a clean bowl and add little amount of water
  6. Mix with your hands and ensure you obtain a uniform consistency
  7. Add more paste of you want a thicker mixture
  8. Simmer for about 5 - 10 minutes
  9. Wash onions and fresh tomatoes and add to the mixture
  10. Once the tomatoes and onions have boiled for a while, remove them and blend
  11. Pour the blended back into the stock
  12. Stir thoroughly
  13. Allow to cook for 8 minutes
  14. Add salt to taste
